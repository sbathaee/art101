Lab 9: JavaScript for the Web

By Kenny Losier and Shayan Bathaee

Challenge

The challenge for this particular lab is to experiment with DOM manipulation. We were asked to create new elements in JavaScript associated with our output div in our HTML file, then display our understanding of changing their attributes and appending them to one another.

Problems

This lab for us was particularly straightforward and simple. However, we did have an issue figuring out a way to initialize two buttons in our JavaScript that would display in our HTML and the local website separately. After we were able to figure that out by debugging and using Professor Modes' slideshow, we had an issue figuring out how to separate the buttons with whitespace and figuring out how to append one button before the other. Taking on Task X was a slight issue for us, but we were able to figure it out fairly quickly on our own! We had some more fun and decided to change the color of the buttons and the colors of the text that appear once each button is clicked!
